The purpose of cookies, how we use them, and how you may manage cookies on our website are all covered by this Cookies Policy.

1. Describe Cookies

When you visit a website, a little text file called a cookie is saved on your computer or mobile device. They are frequently used to increase the functionality and user experience of websites. Cookies come in two varieties: session cookies and persistent cookies

2. Our Cookie Policy

The following are the reasons why we employ cookies:

  • Cookies that are essential to the correct operation of the website: These cookies cannot be deactivated. Typically, they are established in reaction to your actions, such as signing in, changing your privacy choices, or completing forms. Cookies used for website analysis and user behaviour tracking are known as analytical cookies.
  • By doing this, we can enhance the website's functionality and user experience.
  • Cookies that enable better functionality and customisation, such as remembering your preferences or showing tailored information, are known as functional cookies.

3. Your Cookie Choices

You have the option to accept or reject cookies. Most web browsers accept cookies by default, however you can often change your browser's settings to prevent acceptance if you prefer. However, this can restrict you from using the website to its full potential.

4. Cookies from Third Parties

On our website, we could also employ cookies from third parties that are outside of our control. These cookies may gather data about your online activities over time and across other websites for marketing reasons. For additional details, please consult the relevant privacy and cookie policies of these third parties.

3. The Lawfulness of the Processing of Personal Data

We use the following legal justifications to process your personal information:

  • Contract performance: The processing is required to carry out our agreement with you.
  • For the sake of marketing or otherwise furthering our legitimate business interests, we may process your personal information.
  • Your rights and interests will always be balanced and safeguarded by us. Consent: Where required by relevant legislation, we will get your consent before to collecting or processing any of your personal data.

4. Rights of Data Subjects

The following rights are yours under the CCPA and GDPR:

  • Right of access: You have the right to ask to see any personal information we may have on you.
  • Right to rectification: If your personal information is incorrect or lacking, you have the right to seek its rectification.
  • Right to erasure: In some situations, you have the right to ask that your personal data be deleted or removed.
  • Right to restrict processing: You have the right to request the restriction of processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to data portability: You have the right to receive a copy of your personal data in a structured, commonly used, and machine-readable format.
  • Right to object: You have the right to object to the processing of your personal data in certain circumstances.
  • Right to withdraw consent: If we rely on your consent as the legal basis for processing your personal data, you have the right to withdraw your consent at any time.

5. Safety

We are devoted to protecting the security of your data. We have appropriate physical, technological, and administrative safeguards in place to prevent unauthorised access to or disclosure of the information we collect online.

6. Sharing Personal Information

We only allow sharing of your personal information with third parties under the following conditions:

  • With your permission or under your guidance.

  • To safeguard and defend our interests, our possessions, or the security of our business, our personnel, our clients, or others.
  • Your personal information may be transferred to third parties as part of a business transaction, such as a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets.

7. Transfers of Data Internationally

We will make sure the necessary precautions are in place to secure your personal data if we transmit it to nations outside the European Economic Area (EEA) or your jurisdiction.
